Meet Anjali.

When we first met Anjali in 2022, she was nine and had never held a book. Her school had one textbook shared between forty children. Three years and one donated library later, she read out her own poem at the district fair.

The donors who built her library didn't know her name. They saw a campaign, gave ₹500, got a receipt. That's how it works — specific money, specific child, specific change.

Questions donors ask

Are my donations tax-deductible?+

Not yet. We are a community-led foundation and our 80G registration is in progress. You will receive a payment receipt by email immediately. When 80G is granted we will write back to every donor with the update.

Can I give from outside India?+

Our payment partner supports international cards. Please note that formal FCRA clearance for foreign contributions is still being applied for; until then foreign gifts are treated as regular receipts and are not FCRA-reported.

How do you spend my money?+

Almost every rupee goes to programmes on the ground. A small share covers payment gateway fees and essentials like hosting. We publish a spending breakdown per campaign every quarter.

Can I cancel a monthly gift?+

Anytime, from your donor dashboard. Pause or cancel without emailing us. No hard feelings.
